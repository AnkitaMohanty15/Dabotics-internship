About Me

I am a fourth-year Computer Science Engineering student with a strong passion for technology and innovation. Throughout my academic journey, I have been actively involved in various projects and experiences that have shaped my skills and interests.

One of my major areas of focus has been in the field of Machine Learning and Data Science. I have completed several challenging projects in these domains, where I applied my knowledge to solve real-world problems. These experiences have deepened my understanding of data analysis, predictive modeling, and the potential of AI to revolutionize industries.

One of my proudest achievements was participating in a 24-state hackathon, where I collaborated with a diverse team to develop a cutting-edge solution that addressed a pressing social issue. This event not only honed my coding and problem-solving skills but also emphasized the importance of teamwork and effective communication in the tech industry.

Currently, I am on an exciting journey as an intern at Dabotics, a leading robotics company. Here, I am gaining hands-on experience in developing innovative robotic systems and learning about the intersection of robotics, artificial intelligence, and automation.

I believe in the power of continuous learning and staying updated with the latest trends in technology. As I move forward in my career, I am enthusiastic about exploring new challenges and contributing to advancements in the world of computer science and engineering.
